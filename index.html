A long time ago...

EPISODE I

After a few days of struggle configurating the computers, the brave BeCode trainees had managed to take over the wonderful world of Internet.

With her brave companions, Jonathan, Marnie and Quentin, Nathalie worked on a few files and discovered the wonderful universe of Git.

But it was not the end of their adventures, cause in the end, they were capable of much more than it seemed.

The Field was a worrying path to follow, but the Force of the Code was following them along, and there was nothing that could have stopped them from getting better with everyday...

Eventually came the time to take on another challenge : discovering HTML and CSS. The story of a Chinese farmer had to be retold, and the eyesore it was giving every trainee was nothing compared to the challenges awaiting...
